CLUB FRISSON HOTEL & CONVENTION CENTRE

Club Frisson Hotel features elegant accommodations located on landscaped grounds. Offering free parking, it provides access to an outdoor pool and tennis courts.

Enjoy scenic views from the seating area located right beside over-sized windows. Fitted with classic wood furnishings and cable TV, air-conditioned rooms offer the convenience of a mini-bar and free tea/coffee making amenities. Maintaining regular workouts is easy with the well-equipped fitness center and landscaped lawns. After going for a yoga session, relax at the sauna or steam room. Friendly staff can provide tour and business services.

PROPERTY HIGHLIGHTS

The hotel is on the Golden Triangle route that connects Jaipur to New Delhi and Agra. The hotel’s 111 spacious guest rooms & suites offer opulent grandeur; sophisticated amenities provide a comfortable ambience. All room span at least 30 square meter with high ceiling, marble floor and overlook the Amer Fort or Aravali Hills, truly offering a View with every Room. Savour the world of rich Indian cuisine & Local specialties at our Multi-Cuisine Restaurant - Rangmahal, 24 HR in-house dining at - Lotus Pavilion and sample our signature cocktails at our lobby bar Madhushala. In-room dining is available round-the-clock.

Know Your Destination Location Amenities

Know Your Destination

CLUB FRISSON is situated in ‘Amer’– once the capital of the Kacchawaha Rajputs. Located minutes away from the most extravagant forts and palaces, the hotel is walking distance from ‘The Amer Fort’ built in the late 16th Century, this hill fort of Rajasthan is a UNESO World Heritage Site.
